## Projects I'm proud of

### 🎓 [EduReach](https://github.com/SoumyaBadiger-16/edureach-platform) — AI College Chatbot Platform
An agentic AI chatbot built for college students — answers real questions about admissions, fees, placements, and campus life without anyone needing to dig through PDFs or wait for a reply.  
Built with React + TypeScript on the frontend, with an AI pipeline handling intent recognition and dynamic responses.

> **Why it matters:** Most college info systems are broken. Students waste hours finding answers that should take 10 seconds.

---

### 🌾 [Rural Connect / GramSetu](https://github.com/SoumyaBadiger-16/Rural-Connect) — Rural Community Platform
A platform connecting rural communities to services and opportunities, with a premium landing page featuring glassmorphism UI and localized content delivery.

> Shipped a full responsive landing page with cinematic transitions and warm-toned design system — 6,500+ lines changed in one sprint.

---

### 🎙️ [AI Podcast Generator](https://github.com/SoumyaBadiger-16/AI-Podcast-generator)
Generates structured podcast scripts from a topic prompt — useful for content creators who want a starting point without a blank page.

---

### 🗺️ [MCP Learning Path Generator](https://github.com/SoumyaBadiger-16/mcp_learning_path_generator)
Generates personalized learning paths using MCP (Model Context Protocol) — an early experiment with agentic AI design patterns.
